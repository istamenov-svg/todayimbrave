About Us

Bravery isn't born. It's built.

Today, I'm Brave Foundation exists for one reason: to put young people in rooms, on mountains, and in moments where they find out what they're actually made of.

The Foundation

We go where others won't.

Today, I'm Brave is a global nonprofit dedicated to helping young people discover the courage within themselves to face life's challenges and reach their full potential. Founded in 2016, the organization was built on a single conviction: bravery can be unlocked — through experience, mentorship, and creative expression that build confidence, resilience, and community.

The foundation's work has reached young people across the world — from developing a bravery-focused educational curriculum for students in Sierra Leone in the wake of the Ebola crisis, to rebuilding homes for families devastated by natural disasters. Every initiative starts with the same belief: when young people are given the tools to believe in themselves, they can overcome extraordinary obstacles and shape a future no one else imagined for them.

Brave Camp

Not a summer camp. A turning point.

Brave Camp is the foundation's signature program — a weeklong immersive experience that brings this philosophy to life through outdoor challenges, creative expression, and meaningful connections with peers and mentors who refuse to let them play small.

Central to the experience is the Brave Journey™ — a curriculum designed to walk campers through self-discovery, courage-building challenges, and honest reflection. It's not about activities. It's about the moment a kid realizes they just did something they were sure they couldn't. That realization doesn't expire. It rewires how they see themselves — in school, in relationships, in every room they walk into after.

Over the past several years, Brave Camp has welcomed youth from diverse communities while building a growing network of mentors, partners, and supporters who share one commitment: the next generation deserves to know their own strength before the world tries to tell them otherwise.

The Founder

David Angelo

Today, I'm Brave was founded by David Angelo, creative chairman of the advertising agency David&Goliath. Throughout his career, Angelo has built everything around one idea: bravery. His work with global brands and creative communities has centered on helping people face what scares them and step into their authentic purpose.

That same philosophy drove the creation of Today, I'm Brave. Angelo saw what happens when young people discover their own strength early — before doubt, circumstance, or someone else's expectations get in the way. He built a nonprofit to make that discovery possible at scale.

Today, through Brave Camp and partnerships with educators, mentors, and donors, the foundation continues to expand opportunities for young people to learn one of life's most powerful lessons: bravery is not the absence of fear — it's the decision to move forward anyway.
